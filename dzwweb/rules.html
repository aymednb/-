General Rules

  • 1. Respect All Players
    Treat all players with respect. No harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior will be tolerated.
  • 2. No Cheating or Exploits
    Using cheats, hacks, or exploits will result in an immediate permanent ban.
  • 3. Stay In Character
    Maintain roleplay at all times. Breaking character without reason is not allowed.

Roleplay Guidelines

  • 1. Value Your Life
    Act as you would in real life. No unrealistic behavior or suicide attempts.
  • 2. No Random Death Match (RDM)
    Killing players without valid roleplay reason is strictly prohibited.
  • 3. No Vehicle Death Match (VDM)
    Using vehicles as weapons is not allowed unless part of an approved roleplay scenario.

Communication Rules

  • 1. Use Appropriate Language
    No excessive swearing, hate speech, or inappropriate content.
  • 2. Proper Radio Usage
    Use radio channels appropriately and maintain radio discipline.
  • 3. No Metagaming
    Using out-of-character information in-game is strictly prohibited.
